Arduino

Build Real Smart Systems with Arduino

If you are curious about electronics, robotics, or how smart devices work, this diploma is your practical first step into the world of Arduino and embedded systems. You will learn how circuits, code, sensors, and actuators work together, then use them to build real smart projects through a fully hands-on learning experience.

📌 Diploma Overview

Duration: 1 month only
Schedule: 3 Live Sessions per Week
Location: Fully online
Requirements: No need to buy any hardware. Everything is simulated online as if the Arduino is right in front of you.
Prerequisites: No prior experience is needed. We start from the fundamentals.

But here’s the deal:

🚫 This is not for passive learners.
You’ll need focus, commitment, and active participation to make the most of the journey.

📌 What Will You Learn?

• The basics of electricity, circuits, breadboards, and Arduino.
• How to write Arduino code and control inputs and outputs.
• How to work with sensors, motors, LEDs, buzzers, LCDs, relays, and more.
• Problem-solving, algorithms, flowcharts, and debugging techniques.
• How hardware and software work together in real smart systems.
• Basics of communication between devices using protocols such as UART and I2C.
• How to turn an idea into a complete working Arduino project.

📌 Learn by Building

This diploma is built around practice, not just theory. Throughout the course, you will complete hands-on tasks and projects, including:

• Smart parking system.
• Smart irrigation system.
• Smart lighting system.
• Alarm system.
• Calculator using LCD and keypad.
• A final graduation project based on a real-world embedded system idea.

📌 What You’ll Gain

• Practical experience with Arduino and embedded systems.
• Stronger logical thinking and problem-solving skills.
• The ability to connect code with real electronic components.
• A portfolio of practical projects that shows what you can build.
• A Completion certificate that proves your hands-on skills.
• A strong base to continue into robotics, automation, and electronics.

This diploma is more than just an introduction to Arduino. It is a hands-on journey into how smart systems are designed, programmed, and brought to life.
