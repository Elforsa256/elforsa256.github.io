Start Your Artificial Intelligence Journey

Ready to move beyond simply using AI tools and start understanding how artificial intelligence actually works?

The El-Forsa Artificial Intelligence Diploma is a practical, intensive programme designed for students who already have basic Python and logical thinking skills and want to take their first serious step into the world of AI.

Over four weeks, you will learn how to prepare data, train machine learning models, build neural networks, work with image classification and Natural Language Processing, and apply your knowledge through practical assignments and a real-world capstone project.

📌 Diploma Overview:

Duration: 1 month.
Schedule: 3 live sessions per week.
Location: Fully online, allowing you to learn from home.
Requirements: A computer and an internet browser. No complicated software installation is required.
Prerequisites: Basic Python and logical thinking skills.
Admission: Applicants must pass the admission test with a minimum score of 80%.

But here’s the deal:

🚫 This diploma is not for passive learners.
You will need focus, commitment, active participation, and regular practice to complete your journey successfully.

📌 What You’ll Learn:

• Introduction to Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.
• Understanding datasets, features, and labels.
• Cleaning and preparing data for machine learning.
• Handling missing values, duplicates, and incorrect data types.
• Supervised Machine Learning, classification, and regression.
• Training and comparing different machine learning models.
• Evaluating models using accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score.
• Identifying overfitting and underfitting.
• Neural Networks and Deep Learning.
• Convolutional Neural Networks and image classification.
• Natural Language Processing and sentiment analysis.
• Transformers, attention mechanisms, and Large Language Models.
• Transfer Learning and pre-trained AI models.
• Responsible AI, bias, hallucinations, and prompt injection.

📌 Hands-On Practice:

This diploma is highly practical. You will complete activities and projects that develop your skills step by step.

• Weekly coding assignments and challenges.
• Data cleaning and preparation tasks.
• Machine learning experiments using real datasets.
• Model training, evaluation, and comparison exercises.
• Neural network and image classification projects.
• NLP and sentiment analysis activities.
• AI red-teaming exercises to explore the limitations of AI models.
• A final capstone project based on a real-world problem.

You will work with practical, browser-based tools such as Google Colab, Teachable Machine, Hugging Face, and LLM APIs.

📌 What You’ll Gain:

• A strong foundation in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.
• Practical experience working with real datasets.
• The ability to train, test, compare, and evaluate AI models.
• Experience with image classification and Natural Language Processing applications.
• A collection of practical assignments and projects for your portfolio.
• A completed capstone project that demonstrates your skills and creativity.
• A completion certificate recognising your knowledge and practical achievement.

If you are ready to challenge yourself, develop practical AI skills, and understand the technology shaping the future, this diploma is your next step.

Join the El-Forsa Artificial Intelligence Diploma and start building with AI, one smart step at a time.
